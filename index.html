🎮 Basic Commands

!summon – Call monsters to earn CP:

  • 1–3 monsters = +1 to +3 CP
  • Legendary Monster = +100 CP & +10 GCOIN
  • Monster level = Your level −5 to +5

!buff – Support your hero:

  • Normal Buff (+1 CP): Heal, Blessing, Increase Agility
  • Great Buff (+2 CP): Normal Buff + Magnificat, Angelus
  • Epic Buff (+3 CP): >Great Buff + Assumptio, Impositio
  • Legendary Buff (+10 CP): Epic Buff + Kyrie, Gloria, Full Body Protection
  • Ultimate Buff (+25 CP): Legendary Buff +Adrenaline Rush, Weapon Perfection, Over Thrust, Max Over Thrust & +5 GCOIN

!potion – Potion chest outcomes:

  • Red (24%) = +1 CP
  • Yellow (23%) = +2 CP
  • White (23%) = +3 CP
  • Blue (15%) = +10 CP
  • Ygg Seed (10%) = +20 CP
  • Ygg Berry (5%) = +50 CP & +5 GCOIN

⚔️ Join Wave Battles

!joinwave – Randomly Picks difficulty & fight waves:

  • Easy: +10 CP per wave
  • Medium: +20 CP per wave
  • Difficult: +50 CP per wave

5 waves per session • New waves every 30 mins • Check upper-left timer

🧙‍♂️ Curse System

!curse – Risky CP duel with Top 1:

  • Summons 1–4 monsters (Hero+0 to +10)
  • Win: +2× monsters CP / Top 1 loses CP
  • Lose: You lose double / Top 1 gains CP

🌽 Hero Hunts

!hunt – Send hero on a timed mission:

  • 1m = +1 CP • 3m = +3 CP • 5m = +5 CP
  • Max: 60 minutes stacked
  • Earn Hunt Points & climb leaderboard

🗺️ Map Unlocking & Navigation

  • !unlocknextmap – Use CP to unlock next area
  • !yes – Confirm after unlock
  • !warp mapname – Go to any unlocked map
  • !map – View the Star Map
  • !game – Return to Main Game
  • !help – Show Help Menu

⚒️ Equipment Refinement & Gear

!refine weapon – Refine your weapon (10 RP):

  • +1 level per attempt
  • Higher refine = stronger but lower success rate

!refine armor, !refine shield, !refine shoes, !refine muffler,!refine headgear,

  • +1 defense per refine
  • Costs 10 RP per attempt

!gear – View your current gear and refine levels

!mine – Begin mining expedition (Level 50+ only):

  • Spawns monsters that drop RP when defeated
  • Mineral – 1 RP
  • Cobalt Mineral – 2 RP
  • Bradium – 3 RP

🛑 Only if heroes are Level 50 or higher !mine.
