📜 Stream Command Guide

🎮 Basic Commands

!summon – Summon monsters and earn CP:

  • 1 monster = +1 CP
  • 2 monsters = +2 CP
  • 3 monsters = +3 CP
  • Monster level: Your level −5 up to +5

!buff – Empower your hero:

  • Basic: +1 CP
  • Intermediate: +2 CP
  • Ultimate: +3 CP

!potion – Open potion chests:

  • Red Potion = +1 CP
  • Yellow Potion = +2 CP
  • White Potion = +3 CP

⚔️ Join Wave Battles

!joinwave – Choose difficulty and fight waves:

  • Easy: +1 CP per wave
  • Medium: +2 CP per wave
  • Difficult: +3 CP per wave

▪ 3 waves per session.
▪ Waves arrive every 30 minutes.
▪ Registration opens 5 minutes after the previous wave ends.
▪ Refer to the upper-left clock for wave timing.

🧙‍♂️ Curse System

!curse – High-risk CP battle:

  • Summons 1–4 monsters (Level: Hero +0 to +10)
  • If caster wins: +2× monster count CP, Top 1 loses monster count CP
  • If hero dies: Caster loses 2× monster count CP, Top 1 gains monster count CP

🏹 Hero Hunts

!hunt – Send your hero on a timed mission:

  • 1 minute = +1 CP
  • 3 minutes = +3 CP
  • 5 minutes = +5 CP

▪ Stack time up to a max of 60 minutes.
▪ Earn Hunt Points based on total hunt time.
▪ Climb the Hunt Leaderboard!

🗺️ Map Unlocking & Navigation

!unlocknextmap – Unlock the next map in the progression:

  • Consumes the corresponding CP cost required to unlock.
  • Once unlocked, your hero is automatically teleported to the new map.
  • Type !yes to confirm and buy the map after using !unlocknextmap.

!warp mapname – Teleport to any unlocked map. (Not yet implemented)

  • !map – View the Star Map
  • !game – Return to the Main Game
  • !help – Show Help Menu

⚒️ Equipment Refinement & Gear

!refineweapon – Refine your weapon:

  • Each refine adds +1 to weapon level.
  • Higher refine = more damage but lower success rate.
  • Costs CP per refine attempt.

!refinearmor – Refine your armor:

  • Each refine adds +1 to armor level.
  • Higher refine = more defense but lower success rate.
  • Costs CP per refine attempt.

!gear – View your equipment and refine status.

  • Opens the gear window showing your current equips and their refine levels.

🚫 Rules of War

  • No spamming commands.
  • Alternate commands—avoid repeats (e.g., !summon → !buff).
  • Strategic players rise; spammers are ignored.
  • Climb the Leaderboards to earn GCash prizes!
